About Us Page

EXA Marble is the leading Indian conglomerate and a global trendsetter in the world of Natural Stone.


The choice of undertaking the paths less travelled has made all the difference in the success story of EXA Marble. The company was established in a simple warehouse at Kishangarh, Rajasthan, in 1994, and ever since has been raising the bar with its quality products and superior services.


Today, EXA Marble, with a keen aptitude for innovation and skill, inclination towards concurrent technological advancements has emerged as an undisputed leader in marble mining, processing, and research and technology development across the India.


Today we have strong & efficient suppy chain helping us to deliver world class Marble & Granite all over India
